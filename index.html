Vimco Steel Industries

VIMCO Steel: Your Trusted Partner in Metals with Over 30 Years of Excellence With more than three decades of experience in the metals industry, VIMCO Steel is committed to delivering high-quality materials, innovative solutions, and unparalleled customer service. We specialize in catering to diverse metal requirements, including: Aluminum, Copper & Brass, High Nickel Alloys, Stainless Steel, Titanium, Carbon & Alloy Steel .As a global supplier, we stock a wide range of sizes and sections to meet the needs of industries worldwide. Whether you're looking for raw materials for construction, manufacturing, or specialized projects, we serve as a one-stop solution for all your metal requirements.

Markets & Solutions

Steel is vital in emerging sectors, enabling green technologies like carbon capture, hydrogen storage, bioenergy plants and supporting data centers with durable structural frames and cooling systems.

Steel is essential for defense and aerospace, used in armored vehicles, submarines, aircraft, rockets, and military infrastructure providing unmatched strength, durability, and protection in critical applications.

Vimco Steel is strategically located in the country's leading agricultural hub, supplying materials to major global and Indian industries.

Vimco Steel proudly partners with one of the most established productive sectors in the country, contributing to key growth projects for chemical and petrochemical companies.

For both small and large metallurgical projects Vimco Steel provides a wide range of materials to support and drive daily growth.
