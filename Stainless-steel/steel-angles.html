Angles

Welcome to Vimco Steel and Industries

Vimco Steel and Industries is your one-stop destination for high-quality stainless steel angles, crafted to meet the exacting standards of modern industry. Our stainless steel angles are widely recognized for their strength, durability, and versatility in various structural and industrial applications.

Available in grades like 304, 316, 316L, and Duplex 2205, our stainless steel angles are designed to provide superior resistance against corrosion, making them suitable for construction, infrastructure, and heavy-duty frameworks. We adhere to international quality standards like ASTM, BS, JIS, DIN, and AISI to ensure premium product quality.

Our stainless steel angles come in various sizes and finishes, such as brushed, mirror, and matte, tailored to meet your specific project requirements. With a commitment to precision, quality, and customer satisfaction, Vimco Steel is proud to be a leader in the stainless steel industry.
